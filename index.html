      Frequently Asked Questions

      • What is a bank account?
        A bank account is a financial account maintained by a bank where you can deposit, withdraw, or save money.
      • How to transfer money?
        You can transfer money through your bank's mobile app, net banking, or by visiting a branch.
      • What are the fees associated with my account?
        Fees vary by account type. Check with your bank for a detailed list of fees.
      • How can I set up direct deposit to my account?
        To set up direct deposit (ACH) to your account at the Bank, you can fill out a Direct Deposit Authorization form.
      • Do you offer early access to paycheck deposits?
        Yes, with Early Pay, you can get your paycheck up to two days early. To take advantage of Early Pay, you need to have a Momentum® Checking Account or Express Banking® Account that’s set up for direct deposit.
      • How can I provide feedback or report a concern related to my account or my experience with Netbanking?
        We welcome your feedback. If you have a suggestion or concern with your account, you can contact us by phone, online message, visiting a financial center or sending a letter.
      • How do I change my address or phone number?
        You can update your address and phone number by logging into Online Banking and visiting the Service Center link at the top of the page. You can also call us toll free at 1-800-972-3030 or visit a Branch near you.
      • How can I send or receive a wire transfer?
        Visit a financial institution or Bank Branch to set up a wire transfer.
